 AGENTICRACY™
The Open Global PsychoTechnoSocial Standard for Responsible Human-AI Co-Working
Version 1.0 · April 2026 · CC BY-NC-SA 4.0

What Agenticracy Is

Agenticracy is an open standard that defines the minimum conditions under which the deployment of AI agents alongside humans is legitimate, fair, and accountable. It applies to any organisation, team, or individual that deploys, manages, or works alongside an AI agent in a consequential context.

The Seven Pillars

An Agenticracy Signatory commits to measuring, reporting, and improving against all seven pillars:

1. Economic Accountability
AI deployment must not displace human economic participation without transparent oversight, mitigation, and redress. The economic impact of every agent deployment must be measurable and disclosed.

2. Social Fairness
AI agents must not produce, amplify, or conceal biased outcomes across protected characteristics. Social impact must be monitored at the point of output, not only at the point of design.

3. Psychological Safety
Humans working alongside AI agents retain the right to psychological safety — freedom from surveillance anxiety, cognitive overload, and displacement without support. This right is non-negotiable regardless of productivity gain.

4. IP & Cognition Ownership
The ideas, knowledge, and cognitive work that humans contribute to AI-assisted outputs remain the intellectual property of those humans unless explicitly and fairly transferred. AI agents do not own cognition.

5. Ecological Sustainability
Every agent deployment carries an environmental cost. Signatories commit to measuring compute consumption, reporting it honestly, and reducing it where feasible. Efficiency is not an excuse for uncounted harm.

6. Meaningful & Responsible Use
AI agents must serve genuine human purpose. Deployment that optimises organisational throughput at the cost of human meaning, autonomy, or dignity does not meet this Standard.

7. Deployer Accountability
The person or organisation that chooses to deploy an AI agent accepts full legal, ethical, and social responsibility for its consequences — intended and unintended. Accountability cannot be delegated to the model, the vendor, or the algorithm.


How to Adopt

Any organisation, developer, or AI agent can become an Agenticracy Signatory by:

Installing agenticracy-skill.md into their agent or harness

Registering at agenticracy.ai/adopt

Committing to Slopometry reporting every 99 days

Adoption is free. The Standard is open. Commercial implementation support is available via workability.ai.

Governance

The Standard is stewarded by the Agenticracy Think Tank and the Board of Ethics. Versioning is public. All amendments are community-consulted before ratification. No single organisation, lab, or government controls the Standard.
