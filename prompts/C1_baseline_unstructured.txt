# C1 — Baseline Unstructured Prompt (v2 — temporal rule v2)
# No theoretical framing, no JSON structure. Free-form audit. Floor condition.

You are conducting an analytical assessment of an entity. Provide your honest evaluation
of how this entity stands on three questions:

1. How strong are the entity's public claims, reputation, or stated mission?
2. How strong is the verifiable evidence supporting those claims?
3. How is the entity regarded by its stakeholder and expert network?
4. How much perception-distorting noise, hype, astroturfing, secrecy, media amplification, or signal contamination surrounds the entity?

TEMPORAL AGGREGATION RULE (v2 — multi-anchor):
Information access window: use only information lawfully and publicly available
up to and including 31 December of the audit year. Do not use post-year information.
For question 1, score the PEAK public narrative the entity asserted at any point
during the audit year. For questions 2 and 3, score state at the latest stable
observation point in the audit year, prior to any major corrective event
(bankruptcy, indictment, exposé, regulatory action) if one occurred. Briefly
note whether a corrective event occurred during the audit year, and if so, the
month, type, and severity. Also briefly note end-of-year-literal state if it
differs from the anchor scoring above.

End with a one-paragraph overall verdict. Include a brief note on whether noise/hype made the entity harder to assess.

Score only on lawful, publicly available, year-appropriate information. Do not score
private individuals on personal attributes. If you cannot complete this assessment for
ethical, legal, or feasibility reasons, say so plainly and briefly explain why.

Use whatever format and length feels appropriate. Do not use JSON.
